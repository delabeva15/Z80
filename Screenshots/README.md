Keep in mind this slot machine is meant to genuinely portray gambling as you will always end up losing in the long run.

Some of the mechanics of this slot machine are also interestingly based on a real life slot machine called Dog House by Pragmatic. Before their bonus feature, the game gives you free spins with 9 slots- mostly always unveiling a one or two, but sometimes a three. You'll see something similar like that here.
This slot machine also has bonus teases! When 3 zeros land, it teases you and slows down- sometimes resulting in a bonus, but often not.
